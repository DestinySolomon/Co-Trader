Co-Trader Features

Co-Trader is not here to trade for you. It’s here to make sure you don’t trade against yourself. Every feature is built to protect your discipline, alert you on time, and keep your strategy intact.

Discipline Enforcement

Set your own rules — daily loss limits, profit targets, or maximum trades. Once you’ve hit your threshold, Co-Trader steps in, closes your trades, and locks you out until the next session. No more revenge trading or breaking your own system.

Smart Price Alerts

Waiting for EUR/USD to hit 1.25353? Or Bitcoin to touch $110,000? Tell Co-Trader your target, and it will instantly alert you when the market moves. You’ll never miss a setup because you weren’t staring at the charts.

Trade Lock

Fear makes traders close good trades too early. With Trade Lock, once you enter a position, Co-Trader ensures only your Stop Loss or Take Profit decides the outcome. No emotional button-smashing allowed.

Seamless Integration

Co-Trader connects with your exchange or broker account via secure API keys. This way, it can enforce your rules, monitor trades, and send alerts — all without needing to execute trades on your behalf. Your strategy stays yours, but your discipline gets backup.
