Features that keep you disciplined

Co-Trader enforces your own rules, alerts you when the market moves, and locks trades so fear can’t break your plan.

Discipline Enforcer

Auto-close positions or block new trades when your personal rules are hit — stay on plan, not on emotion.

Price Alerts

Set exact price targets and get notified instantly when the market reaches them.

Trade Lock

Prevent fear-driven exits by locking trades until TP/SL is hit.

Secure Connections

Encrypted API key handling, no withdrawals allowed — you stay in control.

Smart Dashboard

Manage rules, monitor alerts, and review history in one simple place.

Integrations

Built to connect with MT4, MT5, and Binance via secure APIs.
