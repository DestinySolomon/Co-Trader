How Co-Trader Works

Co-Trader does three things: enforce your trading rules, alert you at price targets, and lock trades when you choose. Below is a detailed, step-by-step explanation of how each feature works and how Co-Trader enforces discipline without taking control of your funds.

1

Connect Your Account (secure API)

Use your broker/exchange API keys to link Co-Trader. We only ask for the permissions needed — typically read access and trade/close permissions (if you allow enforcement). No withdrawal permission required.

How we keep it safe:
  • Keys are encrypted at rest.
  • Withdrawals are never enabled by default.
  • You can revoke access anytime from your broker dashboard.
Supported: MT4/MT5 (via EA plugin) and exchanges with API keys (e.g., Binance). For MT4/MT5, you'll install our lightweight EA which communicates only with your account.

2

Define Your Rules

Build rules that reflect your trading plan — examples: max daily loss, max trades/day, max position size, or time-based restrictions. Rules are stored on your account and evaluated continuously.

Examples:
  • "Close all positions if daily P&L ≤ -5%."
  • "No new trades after 9PM local time."
  • "Max 2 trades per day."
You can use presets or create custom combinations. Rules can be paused, edited, or scheduled.
3

Monitor Markets & Price Alerts

Set price targets (e.g., EUR/USD 1.25353) or multi-condition alerts. Co-Trader watches live prices and triggers notifications the moment conditions are met — in-app, email, or push.

Alert types:
  • Simple price target
  • Crossing indicators (optional templates)
  • Account condition alerts (e.g., equity drop)
Alerts are synchronous — we use websocket feeds or EA heartbeats for near-instant detection. You receive the alert; you may also instruct Co-Trader to act (if enforcement permission granted).

4

Lock Trades (optional)

When you open a position you can choose to lock it. A locked trade cannot be closed manually from the terminal — it will only exit at the trade's SL or TP unless you perform an approved override (2FA or time-release).

Override options:
  • PIN + 2FA double-confirmation
  • Timed unlock (e.g., unlock after X minutes)
  • Admin override (for prop accounts)
Locking is done locally in the EA (MT4/MT5) or by preventing new orders through the API — Co-Trader does not block withdrawals.
5

Automated Enforcement

If rules are violated (e.g., your daily loss threshold is hit), Co-Trader executes enforcement actions: close positions, block new orders, or lock the account for a cooldown period. Actions and logs are stored for audit.

Enforcement actions:
  • Close all open positions immediately
  • Block new orders until cooldown
  • Notify user with full action log
All actions are timestamped and reversible only via explicit user action (or admin review for prop setups).

Trade with Discipline

With rules enforced, real-time alerts, and optional trade locks, Co-Trader keeps emotion out of trading — leading to consistent adherence to your strategy and better long-term outcomes.

What you get:
  • Accountability to your own rules
  • Clear logs for every enforcement action
  • Peace of mind during volatile markets
Co-Trader is a discipline assistant — you remain in control at all times.
