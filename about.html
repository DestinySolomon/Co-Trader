About CoTrader

CoTrader is not just another tool — it’s your disciplined partner in trading. We’re here to remove emotions, enforce your strategy, and keep you in control whether you trade crypto, forex, or stocks.

Our Mission

We built CoTrader because we know how tough trading can be. From missed opportunities to emotional decisions, traders often struggle to follow their own rules. Our mission is simple: give every trader a reliable companion that enforces discipline, sends timely alerts, and protects their trades.

How We Help You

Smart Alerts

Get instant notifications when your target price is reached — never miss a trade again.

Rule Enforcement

Set your trading rules once and let CoTrader close positions for you when conditions are met.

Trade Lock

Worried about emotional exits? Lock your trades so they can’t be closed prematurely.

Cross-Market Support

From crypto to forex and stocks — CoTrader adapts to your market of choice seamlessly.

Our Story

We started CoTrader after countless nights of frustration, missed trades, and emotional mistakes. As traders ourselves, we realized what was missing wasn’t more signals or flashy tools — it was discipline. CoTrader was born to fill that gap, acting as the voice of reason that traders often need in fast-moving markets.
